About

The commercial real estate web scraper is developed for our sponsor, Capstak. It will be an autonomous scraper that has the ability to scrape various commercial real estate sites and intelligently consolidate the scraped data into a database that Capstak will use as desired. The scraper will not only be useful to Capstak, but also Capstak users as the scraper will aid Capstak in providing recent and relevant real estate data to the end user.
